OUR STORY
Ed Fisher opened Purple Chopstix in 1989. For over 25 years, he’s provided Athens with a unique and eclectic variety of delicious food. These days, his children Amelia and Gabe (left) join him in the kitchen, helping prepare all the Chopstix favorites and crafting new specials weekly.
